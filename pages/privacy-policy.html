Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 July 2023

When you interact with our website, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to share.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Additionally, we automatically collect certain information about your visit to our website, including your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and browsing behavior. This data is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2.1 We utilize the information we collect for the following purposes

Providing Personalized Services: We use your information to deliver and personalize our services according to your preferences.

Communicating with You: We may contact you to respond to your inquiries and provide important information.

Marketing and Promotional Materials: With your consent, we may send you marketing and promotional materials to keep you informed about our products and services.

Improving Website Functionality: We continuously enhance the functionality and performance of our website to provide you with an optimal user experience.

Analyzing and Monitoring Usage: We analyze user behavior and monitor trends to understand how our website is used and make improvements accordingly.

Ensuring Security: We take measures to protect the security and integrity of our systems and prevent unauthorized access or fraud.

Complying with Legal Obligations: We may use your information to meet legal obligations, such as responding to legal processes or government requests.



3. Information sharing

3.1 Rest assured that we have a strict policy of not selling, trading, or renting your personal information to any third parties. Your privacy is our top priority, and we take the necessary steps to protect it. In order to run our business effectively, we may collaborate with trustworthy third-party service providers. When necessary, we may share your information with them, ensuring they adhere to strict privacy standards. These service providers include website hosting, analytics, and marketing service providers. We carefully select these partners to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

3.2 Additionally, we may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process. We may also disclose it to protect our rights or the rights of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

4.1 We are committed to implementing reasonable measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. While we take necessary precautions, it's important to acknowledge that transmitting data over the internet or storing it electronically can never be entirely risk-free. We make every effort to ensure the security of your data; however, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
